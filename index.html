Hey, What's Up?

My name is Mayank Kishore and I am a student at Georgia Tech. Currently, I am exploring the field of Computer Science to discover where I can have the most fun while making the greatest impact.

I love learning. As cheesy as that sounds, it is, for the most part, true. The only notable exception is my high school history class where my teacher insisted on putting an hour long documentry every morning at 7:20am. Unexpectedly, this did not pair well with sleep deprived high school students. But I digress. Learning, is the development of ideas into a concrete understaing of the concepts and usage of the material. Oftentimes, it's easy to forget about the usability of what is taught in class because of the linear nature of lectures. I strive to throughly understand each concept, which leads to failure, which leads to learning, which leads to failure, which leads to a StackOverFlowError. Jokes aside, I am always excited to discover new opportunities to learn. If my resume meets your criteria, feel free to reach out!
