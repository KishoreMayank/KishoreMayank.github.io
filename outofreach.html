Out of Reach Wins

July 2025

It's when you push at something you weren't supposed to do.
It's when your parents worried about all the ways you could fail.
It's when your friends struggled to understand what you were doing.
It's when you saw no way out but still kept digging.
It's when you forget the pain it took to get here.
It's when you go for the next one because it's who you are.

You did it.
Because it was never out of reach.
